What I can do for you

I work across three areas — web development, video editing, and design. Here's a plain breakdown of each, what's included, and who it's best for.

Service 01

Website Development

I build websites that are clean, fast, and mobile-ready. Whether you need a single landing page or a full multi-page site, I handle the design and the code — so you don't need to manage two different people.

I focus on sites that look professional and work well. Not over-engineered, not bloated with features nobody uses. Just something that represents your business well and gets out of the way.

  • Landing pages and business websites
  • Portfolio and personal brand sites
  • E-commerce (Shopify / WooCommerce)
  • WordPress, Webflow, or custom HTML/CSS
  • Mobile responsive and fast-loading
  • Contact forms and basic SEO setup
⚡ Typical delivery: 3–14 days depending on scope

🎁 First video FREE for new clients

Video Editing

You shoot the footage, I turn it into something worth watching. I edit for social media — Reels, Shorts, TikToks — and longer content like event highlights or brand videos.

I keep edits tight, add subtitles where needed, pick music that fits, and make sure the pacing works for the platform. If you're posting regularly, I can also handle batches so you're never scrambling for content.

  • Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts
  • TikTok edits with trending formats
  • Captions and animated subtitles
  • Colour grading and basic effects
  • Event highlights and brand videos
  • Music selection and audio cleanup
⚡ Typical delivery: 2–5 days per video
🎬
Video Editing
Reels · Shorts · Highlights · Captions
Service 03

Graphic Design

Need something designed? I handle the day-to-day design tasks that businesses actually need — flyers, menus, invoices, social graphics. Clean, on-brand, and ready to use.

I work fast on this type of project. Most straightforward design jobs are turned around within 24–48 hours. If you need something more involved or a batch of assets, I'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

  • Promotional posters and flyers
  • Restaurant and service menus
  • Branded invoices and letterheads
  • Invitation and event cards
  • Social media graphics and templates
  • Print-ready and digital formats
⚡ Typical delivery: 24–48 hours for most designs
